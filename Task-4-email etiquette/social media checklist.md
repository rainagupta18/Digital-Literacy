Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

 DO’s (Responsible Social Media Use)
- Think before posting and verify information before sharing.
-	Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
-	Keep your social media accounts private whenever possible.
-	Be respectful and polite in comments and messages.
-	Share positive, useful and educational content.

 DON’Ts (Unsafe Social Media Behaviour)
-	Do not share personal information like phone number, address, OTP or passwords.
- Do not post fake news, rumors or unverified content.
- Avoid accepting friend requests from strangers.
-	Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments.
-	Never click suspicious links or unknown advertisements.
